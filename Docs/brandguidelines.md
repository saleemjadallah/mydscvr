## **7. Imagery & Photography Guidelines**

### **Hero Images**

**Style:**
- Professional yet authentic, polished but not overly posed 
- Show diverse professionals (various ages, ethnicities, industries)
- High-quality lighting with natural feel 
- Confident, approachable expressions

**Composition:**
- Grid layouts showing variety (3x3 or 4x4 grids)
- Before/after comparisons (split screen or slider)
- Platform mockups showing headshots in context  (LinkedIn, resume, website)

**Colors in Photos:**
- Navy blue, charcoal, gray for professional/business contexts 
- Variety of skin tones and backgrounds to show inclusivity 
- Avoid overly bright or neon colors that clash with brand palette

### **Template Preview Images**

Each style template needs:
1. **Preview card** - showing what the template looks like
